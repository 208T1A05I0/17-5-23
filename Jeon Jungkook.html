Jeon Jungkook

Jeon Jeong-Guk, better known by his stage name BTS Jungkook, is a multitalented musician from South Korea. He serves as the lead singer, dancer, and rapper for the internationally acclaimed musical group Bangtan Sonyeondan, or BTS. As the youngest member of the group, which also features Kim Taehyung, Rap Monster, Jimin, Jin, Suga, and J-Hope. Jungkook has gained a massive following among young audiences thanks to his exceptional talent, particularly his rapping and dancing skills.

                 

Early life and education

Jeon Jung-kook (Korean: 전정국) was born on September 1, 1997, in Busan, South Korea.His family consists of his parents and an elder brother.He attended Baekyang Elementary and Middle School in Busan. When he became a trainee, he transferred to Singu Middle School in Seoul.Jungkook initially had dreams of becoming a badminton player when he was younger, but after seeing G-Dragon perform "Heartbreaker" on television, it influenced him to want to become a singer. In 2011, Jungkook auditioned for the South Korean talent show Superstar K during its auditions in Daegu.Though he was not selected, he received casting offers from seven entertainment companies. He eventually chose to become a trainee under Big Hit Entertainment after seeing RM, now his fellow band member and leader in BTS, perform. To work on his dance skills in preparation for debut, he went to Los Angeles during the summer of 2012 to receive dance training from Movement Lifestyle. In June 2012, he appeared in Jo Kwon's "I'm Da One" music video and also worked as a backup dancer for Glam before his debut. He graduated from School of Performing Arts Seoul, an arts high school, in 2017. In November 2016, he decided to forgo taking the CSATs, Korea's nationwide university entrance exam. In March 2022, he received his degree from Global Cyber University's Department of Broadcasting and Entertainment. He was awarded the President's Award, the school's highest honor.

Career

At the age of just 13, BTS Jungkook auditioned for the TV talent show ‘Superstar K’, but unfortunately, he was not selected. However, he did not give up and received offers from eight different talented organizations before ultimately being signed by Big Hit Entertainment. At the age of 14, he began learning dance from Movement Lifestyle, and in 2013, he joined BTS and made his debut with the song ‘No More Dream’ for their album ‘2 Cool 4 Skool’. Since then, Jungkook has released numerous hit songs and albums with his bandmates and has performed in many stage shows, touring several countries. The band’s success is evident from their massive fan following and worldwide popularity, as well as the numerous awards they have received, including the Melon Music Award, Golden Disk Award, Seoul Music Award, and Bonsang Award. In addition to his work with BTS, Jungkook has collaborated with other Korean artists and political leaders on the song ‘One Dream One Korea’, which highlights the connection between South Korea and North Korea and the desire for the unification of the two countries. He also appeared in Jo Kwon’s music video for ‘I’m Da One’ in 2012. Jungkook has also made several appearances on TV shows, including three episodes of ‘BTS China Job’ in 2014, as well as ‘Flower Boy Bromance’, ‘Special MC in Music Core’, ‘National Idol Singing Contest’, and the reality program ‘Flower Crew’ in 2016, alongside Jo Se-ho, Yoo Byung-Jae, and Ki Min-Seok.
